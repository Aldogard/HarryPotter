Explanations

  • Energy Usage indicates the required energy to perform the melee. A melee can only be performed if the wizard has enough energy remaining.
  • Most Melees are able to inflict damage on the opposing Wizard. Max Damage indicates the maximum amount of damage that can be inflicted.
  • Stunned indicates whether the Melee can stun the opposing wizard or not. The use of some Potions can protect against the stun effect.
  • Confunded indicates whether the Melee can confound the opposing wizard or not. The use of some Potions can protect against the confound effect.
  • Experience indicates the number of victories a wizards needs in order to use the Melee.
