Explanations

  • Healing indicates the percentage of the initial Healthpoints, that is restored by using the Potion. 0 indicates that this potion cannot restore health. Some Potions can even decrease the health of a wizard.
  • Energy indicates the percentage of the initial Energy, that is restored by using the Potion. 0 indicates that this potion cannot restore energy.
  • Anti Paralysis indicates whether the use of the Potion protects against stun effects.
  • Anti Confunded indicates whether the use of the Potion protects against confound effects.
  • Some Potions are able to inflict damage on the opposing Wizard. Max Damage indicates the maximum amount of damage that can be inflicted. However, most Potions are only affecting the user.
  • Additional Factor (AF) increases the Factor of the Wizard times the indicated Factor when the Potion is used. 1 indicates that there is no increase.
  • Experience indicates the number of victories a wizards needs in order to use the Potion.
