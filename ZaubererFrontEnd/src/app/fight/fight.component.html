The Prolog

The Wizarding World is in great turmoil once again. Just as in the Great Fight, Hogwarts is the last remaining outpost of the Resistance. According to the latest intelligence of the Resistance an unknown number of opposing Wizards is on its way to Hogwarts.

The defences are prepared and the remaining wizards spread at the entrances. One brave Wizard stands up to defend the vital entrance at the Main-Gate. Can the Wizard hold off the attack?
