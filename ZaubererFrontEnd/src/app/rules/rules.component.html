Rules for the Fight

  1. The Attackers decide where to attack and therefore, the environment was choosen randomly
  2. All participating Wizards have to fight, except when the Defender is defeated early
  3. The fight between to Wizards can end neither prematurely nor in a tie
  4. The two opposing factions of Defenders and Attackers do not change during the fight
  5. The Defender has to defeat all Attackers to win the fight
  6. An Attacker will receive a victory even if he did not fight
  7. The Attackers fight the Defender in the order they are selected
  8. The Wizard can only use a Spell or activate an Animal if they have sufficient experience and energy
  9. Potions do not require energy in order to be used but a Wizard still has to have sufficient experience
  10. The maximum Damage of an Attack is always factored with the factor of the attacking Wizard and a random factor between 0 and 1
  11. The maximum Damage of a Potion or Animal attack is always factored a random factor between 0 and 1
  12. Additionally, there are strengths and weaknesses. If the Wizard's class has especial strength or weakness against the opposing Wizard, the random factor will be doubled or halved respectively.
  13. The effects stunned, confunded and protego have a 50 percent chance of working
  14. The effect anti Paralysis and anti Confunded have a 50 percent chance of working
  15. The Wizards who are currently fighting are alternating turns. The only exception being when one Wizard has been stunned
  16. Each turn, a Wizard will receive 3 Energy even if he or she is stunned.

Please confirm the rules to start the Fight
