Rules for the Fight

  1. The Attackers decide where to attack and therefore, the environment was choosen randomly
  2. All participating wizards have to fight, except when the defender is defeated early
  3. The fight can end neither prematurely nor in a tie
  4. The two opposing factions of defenders and attackers do not change during the fight
  5. The defender has to defeat all attackers to win the fight
  6. An attacker will receive a victory even if he did not fight
  7. The attackers fight the defender in the order they are selected
  8. The wizard can only use a Spell or Potion if they have sufficient experience and energy
  9. The Max Damage of an Attack or Potion is always factored with the factor of the attacking wizard and a random factor between 0 and 1
  10. The effects stunned, confunded and protego have a 50 percent chance of working
  11. The effect anti Paralysis and anti Confunded have a 50 percent chance of working

Please confirm the rules to start the Fight
