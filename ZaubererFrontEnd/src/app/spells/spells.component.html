Explanations

  • Energy Usage indicates the required energy to perform the spell. A spell can only be performed if the wizard has enough energy remaining.
  • Most Spells are able to inflict damage on the opposing Wizard. Max Damage indicates the maximum amount of damage that can be inflicted. Caution, some Spells have some special features, which make them especially useful or especially dangerous!
  • Stunned indicates whether the Spell can stun the opposing wizard or not. The use of some Potions can protect against the stun effect.
  • Confunded indicates whether the Spell can confound the opposing wizard or not. The use of some Potions can protect against the confound effect.
