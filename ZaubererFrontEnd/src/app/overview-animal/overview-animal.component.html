Explanations

  • Energy Usage indicates the required energy to put the animal into action. An animal can only be activated if the wizard has enough energy remaining.
  • Healing indicates the percentage of the initial Healthpoints, that is restored by using the Animal. 0 indicates that this Animal cannot restore health.
  • Energy indicates the percentage of the initial Energy, that is restored by using the Animal. 0 indicates that this Animal cannot restore energy.
  • Most Animals are able to inflict damage on the opposing Wizard. Max Damage indicates the maximum amount of damage that can be inflicted.
  • Experience indicates the number of victories a wizards needs in order to tame an animal.
